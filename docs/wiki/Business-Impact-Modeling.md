# Business Impact Modeling

Architecture decisions are ultimately business decisions. EAIS provides the financial transparency needed to justify technical investments.

## 💰 Financial Insights
- **TCO Modeling**: Total Cost of Ownership over 1, 3, and 5 years.
- **ROI Calculation**: Estimated return on investment based on productivity gains and operational efficiency.
- **Cloud Spending Estimate**: Projected monthly recurring costs for the selected cloud services.

## 📈 Impact Scoring
Beyond costs, EAIS evaluates:
- **Agility**: How quickly the design allows for changes.
- **Risk Level**: Technical and operational risk score.
- **Sustainability**: Estimated carbon footprint impact (Cloud-carbon-footprint integration).
- **Time-to-Market**: Estimated project duration based on complexity.
